Hunt Outside

Instructions

Below is a map for this outdoor scavenger hunt. Right now, the map has 3 puzzles for you to solve. Take a good look at the map and try to figure out where you need to go.

Once you think you are close, look for a little QR code hanging around. If you can't find it, click on the puzzle's location hint. Once you find the QR code, scan it with an app on your phone, and it will lead you to the puzzle page. Go to the puzzle and complete it!

When you've found all the QR codes and finished all of the puzzles, scroll to the bottom of the page and click "I'm all done!".

Good luck!
