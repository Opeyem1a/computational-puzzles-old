Pathfinder!

This is a map, laid out as a grid like a chess board. Let's suppose you are standing in the block with the smiley face at the bottom left corner. You can move from one block to the next, using one of the following moves:

Possible moves:

- Move forward in the direction you are facing by 1 block.

- Turn left by 90⁰, without advancing forward.

- Turn right by 90⁰, without advancing forward.

Example:

We've copied the original map to show you an example of how you can navigate on this map. Let's say you were given the following path:

Starting from the bottom left corner, the first step is to move forward and the second step is to turn right. At this point, you can see the map has a smiley face turned sideways to the right.

This path leads us to block "B" as shown in the example. Cool, right? Now, let's use our pathfinding skills to find the hidden treasure!
