Routes!

The picture you see on the right is called a "graph". Let's say this graph is an abstract representation of your neighbourhood. Your house is located at the house icon (). The other 5 circles show the houses of your friends in the neighbourhood: Ann, Bob, Cam, Dan, and Eve.

The lines in between the houses show possible roads you can take to go from one house to another. Lastly, the numbers along each line tells you the difficulty of each road — e.g., steeper and longer roads have a higher number because they use up more of your energy.

Your quest here is to find routes between houses so you minimize the amount of energy required to get to your friend's house.

Example

You are at home and you want to visit your friend Ann. There are many ways to get to her house. Here are 2 potential routes to consider.

Route 1: → Ann's House (Uses 3 energy)

Route 2: → Cam's House → Ann's House (Uses 2 energy)

Even though route 2 requires you to take two roads, the total amount of energy spent is less than what you would need to go through route 1. So route 2 wins!

Quest

What is the minimum amount of energy you need to spend to go from your house to Dan's house?
